**Rennard de Oliveira Brito** is an MSc researcher at the **University of São Paulo (USP)** focusing on **distribution network expansion planning** with **OpenDSS** and **Python-based** frameworks. He develops methods grounded in **NSGA-II** to tackle **energy losses**, **reverse power flow**, and **voltage violations** in Brazilian distribution systems.

He currently works at **Enel Brasil** on the **technical losses** team, contributing Python tools for **network analysis** and **optimization**.
